Role: Leader, Main Rapper, Producer, Songwriter
Name: Kim Namjoon (김남준)
BIRTHDAY: September 12, 1994 (age: 27)
Born in: Ilsan, South Korea
Height: 181 cm
Weight: 67 kg

RM is the leader of BTS and he played a big part in helping Big Hit form the group that fans get to enjoy today.

He’s a low-key genius with an IQ of 148 and taught himself English by watching episodes of Friends. RM is also a writer and producer for the band and has also put out two of his own solo albums titled Mono. and RM.

In their early days, RM struggled a lot with his identity as a musician. After his career as an underground rapper, he received a lot of backlash from the community for joining BTS. Meanwhile, he received a lot of criticism from the Korean music industry that he didn’t fit the beauty standards for an idol group.
In his spare time, you can find him Namjooning – spending time in nature, riding his bike, and visiting art museums.
