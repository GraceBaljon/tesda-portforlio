RMclose

Role: Leader, Main Rapper, Producer, Songwriter
Name: Kim Namjoon (김남준)
BIRTHDAY: September 12, 1994 (age: 27)
Born in: Ilsan, South Korea
Height: 181 cm
Weight: 67 kg

RM is the leader of BTS and he played a big part in helping Big Hit form the group that fans get to enjoy today.

He’s a low-key genius with an IQ of 148 and taught himself English by watching episodes of Friends. RM is also a writer and producer for the band and has also put out two of his own solo albums titled Mono. and RM.

In their early days, RM struggled a lot with his identity as a musician. After his career as an underground rapper, he received a lot of backlash from the community for joining BTS. Meanwhile, he received a lot of criticism from the Korean music industry that he didn’t fit the beauty standards for an idol group.
In his spare time, you can find him Namjooning – spending time in nature, riding his bike, and visiting art museums.

Role: Visual, Sub Vocal, Madhyung (맏형)
Name: Kim Seokjin (김석진)
BIRTHDAY: December 4, 1992 (age: 29)
Born in: Gwacheon, South Korea
Height: 179 cm
Weight: 63 kg

Jin is the oldest BTS member, although he doesn’t often act like it. He’s known for his dad jokes, describing himself as “Worldwide handsome” in response to the times his pictures have gone viral, and winking or blowing kisses to the camera whenever it lands on him.

Despite being the oldest, Jin is one of the more easily frightened members. He can also be quite dramatic when he’s scared and it makes for a lot of great entertainment for fans on video content. He’s also one of the better cooks in the band and has a series of videos titled “Eat Jin” that focuses on his foodie adventures.

Jin was originally studying acting and was infamously spotted on the streets by a Big Hit Entertainment staff member. Though he’s never formally acted in any K-dramas, you can easily see how natural his acting skills are in many of the BTS music videos.

SUGAclose

Role: Lead Rapper, Producer, Songwriter
Name: Min Yoongi (민윤기)
BIRTHDAY: March 9, 1993 (age: 28)
Born in: Daegu, South Korea
Height: 176 cm
Weight: 59 kg

Suga is the second oldest BTS member and can often be found taking care of things for the other members.

He’s known for being a bit of an introvert which sometimes leads people to wrongly believe his personality is cold. When actually Suga is very caring and sensitive when you get to know him better. The other members like to joke about how he prefers to help them without anyone noticing. And when he gets really excited his silly side will shine right through.

He’s well known for working long hours in his studio but when he has time off he likes to sleep as much as possible. Suga has been outspoken about his struggles with mental health in hopes to help others open up too.
Prior to joining Big Hit Entertainment, Suga was an underground rapper and jokes that he was tricked into joining BTS when he initially thought he would just be a producer. He’s also spoken about his financial instability growing up and how he’d often go without eating when he needed money to take the bus.

Name: Jung Hoseok (정호석)
BIRTHDAY: February 19, 1994 (age: 27)
Born in: Gwangju, South Korea
Height: 177 cm
Weight: 65 kg

J-Hope is the dance leader of the group and is also the sunshine of the group.
Having been an underground hip-hop dancer, he prides himself with being the dance teacher of the group during dance practice and can spot mistakes from a mile away which the members have admitted they get scared of. But praises them greatly when they make great effort to do their dances.

J-Hope is known for being quite silly pretty much wherever he is because he likes to help others laugh and have a good time. If you watch him in interviews, he will most definitely introduce himself as “I’m your hope, you’re my hope, I’m J-Hope.” If you’re ever in a bad mood, all you need to do is watch some J-Hope videos and your day will get 100% better.

J-Hope is known to love fashion and loves to go shopping. He’s not afraid to try different styles and loves having bags as accessories.

JIMINclose

Role: Main Dancer, Lead Vocalist
Name: Park Jimin (박지민)
BIRTHDAY: October 13, 1995 (age: 26)
Born in: Busan, South Korea
Height: 175 cm
Weight: 61 kg

Jimin had the shortest amount of time as a trainee before BTS debuted and almost didn’t make the cut. Although he’s a formally trained dancer, he had no experience with singing and had to continue to train hard over the years on his vocals. These days, he seems a lot more satisfied with his skills and has no problem hitting all the right notes live.

Jimin is perhaps one of the most affectionate members, towards his fellow bandmates and fans.

Along with being affectionate, he’s also extremely caring about the other members. If one of them gets upset on stage, Jimin is usually the first one to rush to their side to try and cheer them up. He’s always selfless and seems to constantly put others before himself.

Vclose

Role: Lead Dancer, Sub Vocalist, Visual
Name: Kim Taehyung (김태형)
BIRTHDAY: December 31, 1995 (age: 26)
Born in: Daegu, South Korea
Height: 179 cm
Weight: 62 kg

V comes from a family of farmers and has said that he probably would have ended up doing the same thing if he hadn’t joined BTS. He remains extremely close with his family and greatly looks up to his father.

One word to describe V would be: unique. His voice easily stands out from the other vocal line because of his soulful and deep tone. He loves to play around with fashion and has a great appreciation for art as well.

He’s well known for his outgoing personality, and it’s often joked that he can become friends with anyone that he meets. His fun and silly personality often comes out with fan engagements as well.

After having a close relationship with his grandmother, V has said he picked up his fashion sense from her.
Along with photography, V likes to paint in his free time.
V came up with the term “I purple you” which is used by BTS and ARMYs as another way to say I love and trust you.

JUNGKOOKclose

Role: Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer, Sub Rapper, Center, Maknae
Name: Jeon Jungkook (전정국)
BIRTHDAY: September 1, 1997 (age: 24)
Born in: Busan, South Korea
Height: 178 cm
Weight: 70 kg

Jungkook is the youngest BTS member, also known as their Golden Maknae because he seems to be good at basically everything. Along with his vocal and dancing skills, Jungkook is also quite athletic and artistic.

Initially, Jungkook left his hometown at a very young age to take part in a talent show for kids and was soon scouted by several entertainment agencies. He ended up signing on with Big Hit after being impressed by RM.

By the time the group did debut, he was just 15 years old. Since he left home at such a young age, Jungkook often says that the other members basically raised him and had a huge impact on who he is today.

He’s also well known for his video productions which are released under the name of G.C.F (Golden Closet Films) and is fascinated with cameras. He loves to film the other members and edit them in his free time.
In their music video 'Life Goes On', Jungkook was one of the directors for it.
