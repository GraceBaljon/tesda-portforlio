HELLO WE ARE BTS!


BTS is a 7 member group from south Korea.They debuted last June 2013 and have celebrated their 8th anniversary last June 2021. Their members, RM, Jin, Suga, J-hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook, joined the then entertainment company called BigHit entertainment, and now called HYBE, and trained their skills of singing dancing and rapping until they were ready to debut. In a span of 8 years, they have won 404 awards,20 artist of the year award from 10 different award shows since 2016 to present. They debuted in the U.S. last 2017 when they were invited to attend the billboard music awards after being nominated top social artist and defeating Justin Bieber.
