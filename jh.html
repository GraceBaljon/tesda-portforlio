Role: Main Dancer, Sub Rapper, Sub Vocalist, Producer, Songwriter, Sunshine
Name: Jung Hoseok (정호석)
BIRTHDAY: February 19, 1994 (age: 27)
Born in: Gwangju, South Korea
Height: 177 cm
Weight: 65 kg

J-Hope is the dance leader of the group and is also the sunshine of the group.
Having been an underground hip-hop dancer, he prides himself with being the dance teacher of the group during dance practice and can spot mistakes from a mile away which the members have admitted they get scared of. But praises them greatly when they make great effort to do their dances.

J-Hope is known for being quite silly pretty much wherever he is because he likes to help others laugh and have a good time. If you watch him in interviews, he will most definitely introduce himself as “I’m your hope, you’re my hope, I’m J-Hope.” If you’re ever in a bad mood, all you need to do is watch some J-Hope videos and your day will get 100% better.

J-Hope is known to love fashion and loves to go shopping. He’s not afraid to try different styles and loves having bags as accessories.
